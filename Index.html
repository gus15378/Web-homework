Project Summary

In this project, I tried to analyze weather patterns (Temperature, humidity, cloudiness & windspeed) base on the cities location from the equator. To accomplish this analysis, we collect data from the OpenWeatherAPI to create datasets of over 500 cities.

Once the data is assembled, I used Matplotlib to plot different measures of weather vs latitute including Temperature, humidity, cloudiness & windspeed. This site provide visualization of the data as well as analysis of each measure.
