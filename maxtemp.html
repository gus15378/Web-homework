Maximum Temperature

Based on our data, the cities closer to the equator (0 degree Latitude) has higher Maximum temperature than those further away. We also observe a number of outliers that has significantly higher maximum temperature than other cities at the same latitude.

We should also note that the scatter plot seems to roughly follow a parabolic shape with more cities focused at the higher latitude.
